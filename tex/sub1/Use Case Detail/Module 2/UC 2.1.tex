Use Case ID & 2.1 \\
\hline
Use Case Name & Realtime park guidance for commuters \\
\hline
Actors & Primary: Learners, Faculty Member \& Staff, Temporary Visitors. \par Supporting: Signages. \\
\hline
Description & Main users and Temporary visitors get guidance via the parking signages. The available slots for each way/area is displayed on the signages. \\
\hline
Trigger & A commuter arrives at the campus parking entrances or drives past key intersections. \\
\hline
Pre-conditions & IoT parking sensors and local gateways are online. Signages are currently working. \\
\hline
Post-conditions & The commuter successfully parks in an available slot, and the system updates the real-time availability on its signage. \\
\hline
Main Flow & 
1. The commuter arrives at the entrance of the parking lot. \par
2. The commuter observes signage at the entrance which displays availability (full, almost full, available). \par
3. The commuter enters the parking facility. \par
4. At intersections, electronic signage provides directional information and available slots. \par
5. The commuter finds the parking spot. \par
6. IoT sensor detects new occupancy and relays information through local gateways; the system updates signages and maps. \\
\hline
Alternate Flows & 
\textbf{A1: Full Facility (at step 2):} Signage displays zero slots available. Gateway declines to open (UC 1.8). The commuters are redirected to other parking zones of HCMUT. \par \vspace{0.5em}
\textbf{A2: Zone Full En Route (at step 4):} While driving towards Zone A, other vehicles occupy the remaining spots. The system detects the change, recalculates the nearest available zone (e.g., Zone B), and updates the Directional Signage to redirect the commuter. The commuter follows the updated directions and resumes Step 5. \\
\hline
Exception Flows & 
\textbf{E1: Hardware/Connection Failure:} The system detects a loss of connection or data timeout. If sensors fail, the system excludes those slots and resyncs data. If the entire system fails, signages fallback to error mode, guiding commuters to follow parking operators' instructions. The system triggers an alert to the IT Team via the Web App (UC 2.4). \\
